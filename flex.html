GROUP TOURS

Other than normal tours these are the different categories of group tours also known as mass tours

A group tour is an opportunity to explore a destination in the company of like-minded travellers and is a great way to see the world.

Educational tours

An educational tour offers students of all Classes an opportunity for a rich immersion experience. While an educational tour is great fun, it differs from a vacation. Students come to appreciate parts of the world on a much deeper level than they ever could in the classroom. Students converse and socialize with local people, which broadens their perspective greatly.

Pilgrimage tours

Pilgrimage tourism is essentially the process of visiting pilgrimage sites. These are primarily religious destinations, and said to have formed a very early version of tourism. Typically, pilgrimages are long journeys taken over days, weeks or even months for religious purposes.

Culinary tours

Culinary tourism or food tourism or gastronomy tourism is the exploration of food as the purpose of tourism. It is considered a vital component of the tourism experience
